Materials & Finishes

Substrates and finishes available for commission.

Every material listed below is available for specification into your project. Samples available on request for trade accounts.

Brushed Brass

Premium brass substrate with a directional brushed finish. Ideal for hospitality signage, architectural accents, and high-end wayfinding. Ages beautifully in situ.

  • Thickness: 1.5mm – 3mm
  • Finish: Brushed, Mirror, Patinated
  • Lead time: 5–10 business days

Anodized Aluminium

Lightweight and durable. Available in silver, black, gold, and bronze anodized finishes. The preferred substrate for commercial wayfinding and bar fitout elements.

  • Thickness: 1.5mm – 6mm
  • Finish: Silver, Black, Gold, Bronze
  • Lead time: 5–10 business days

Stainless Steel

Grade 304 stainless for exterior-rated and high-traffic applications. Brushed or mirror finish. Suited to building identification, lobby directories, and outdoor signage.

  • Thickness: 1.5mm – 3mm
  • Finish: Brushed, Mirror
  • Lead time: 7–12 business days

Frosted & Cast Acrylic

Clean, contemporary finish for interior signage and branded elements. Available in clear frosted, solid white, black, and custom tints. Backlit-compatible options available.

  • Thickness: 3mm – 10mm
  • Finish: Frosted, Solid, Tinted
  • Lead time: 5–8 business days

Hardwoods

American oak, black walnut, and bamboo for warm, tactile pieces. Used in awards, desk nameplates, and bespoke hospitality items. FSC-certified stock available.

  • Species: Oak, Walnut, Bamboo
  • Finish: Natural, Oiled, Stained
  • Lead time: 5–10 business days

Glass

Toughened and laminated glass for luxury residential and boutique commercial applications. Sandblasted or deep-etched finish for high-contrast results on doors and partitions.

  • Thickness: 4mm – 12mm
  • Finish: Sandblasted, Deep Etched
  • Lead time: 10–15 business days
