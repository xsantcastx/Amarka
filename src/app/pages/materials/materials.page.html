Materials

The substrates we work with — and what each one does best.

All six are available for specification into your project. Physical samples shipped on request for trade account holders.

Brushed Brass

Directional brushed finish with warm undertones. The go-to for hospitality signage, architectural accents, and high-end wayfinding — develops a richer patina over time.

  • Thickness: 1.5mm – 3mm
  • Finish: Brushed, Mirror, Patinated
  • Lead time: 5–10 business days

Anodized Aluminium

Lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and available in four anodized tones. The preferred substrate for commercial wayfinding systems and bar fitout elements where durability matters.

  • Thickness: 1.5mm – 6mm
  • Finish: Silver, Black, Gold, Bronze
  • Lead time: 5–10 business days

Stainless Steel

Grade 304 stainless — exterior-rated and built for high-traffic environments. Brushed or mirror finish. Ideal for building identification, lobby directories, and outdoor signage that needs to last.

  • Thickness: 1.5mm – 3mm
  • Finish: Brushed, Mirror
  • Lead time: 7–12 business days

Frosted & Cast Acrylic

Clean, contemporary material for interior signage and branded elements. Available in frosted, solid, and custom tints — with backlit-compatible options for illuminated applications.

  • Thickness: 3mm – 10mm
  • Finish: Frosted, Solid, Tinted
  • Lead time: 5–8 business days

Hardwoods

American oak, black walnut, and bamboo — warm and tactile. Used in awards, desk nameplates, and bespoke hospitality pieces. FSC-certified stock available on request.

  • Species: Oak, Walnut, Bamboo
  • Finish: Natural, Oiled, Stained
  • Lead time: 5–10 business days

Glass

Toughened and laminated glass for luxury residential and boutique commercial settings. Sandblasted or deep-etched for high-contrast engraving on doors, partitions, and feature walls.

  • Thickness: 4mm – 12mm
  • Finish: Sandblasted, Deep Etched
  • Lead time: 10–15 business days
