About Amarka

A Stamford studio built around one thing: engraving done right.

We work directly with interior designers, general contractors, and hospitality groups — fabricating signage, wayfinding, awards, and architectural accents to spec on six substrates.

The Studio

Precision is not a feature. It is the entire point.

Amarka is a laser engraving studio in Stamford, Connecticut, purpose-built for the Connecticut and tri-state trade. We serve interior designers, general contractors, hospitality groups, and corporate operators who need engraved elements fabricated to spec — on material, on time, and to a standard that belongs in the spaces they create.

Every commission starts with a conversation and ends with a finished piece that meets the brief exactly. No catalogue. No off-the-shelf. Just precision engraving across six substrates, delivered within 5–10 business days.
